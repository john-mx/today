Advice from the Rangers

  • Have warm clothing. Weather can change rapidly, and you might be out later than you planned.
  • Check out the new tribes exhibit in the 29 Palms Visitor Center.
  • Start your youngsters out as Junior Rangers at any of the Visitor Centers.
  • Popular trails like Hidden Valley, Barker Dam may have full parking lots from 10 to 2pm. Try to come earlier or later.
  • Keys View gets very crowded near sunset. There are many beautiful views in the park that are more accessible.
  • When popular trails are crowded, try a section of a longer trail, such as Boy Scout Trail or the California Hiking and Riding Trail.

  • And finally: Do Not Die Today! Be Safe.

Fire Information

Current Fire Level: Moderate

When the fire danger is "moderate" it means that fires can start from most accidental causes, but the number of fire starts is usually pretty low. If a fire does start in an open, dry grassland, it will burn and spread quickly on windy days. Most wood fires will spread slowly to moderately. Average fire intensity will be moderate except in heavy concentrations of fuel, which may burn hot. Fires are still not likely to become serious and are often easy to control.
